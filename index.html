Unlock the power of Operations Manager!

To get the most out of your Operations Manager environment you will sooner or later have the need to create your own management packs. And there is no denying it, VSAE is quite hard. There is alot of XML.But with the MP Development Toolkit you get the full power of Visual Studio but in a much easier and faster way.

The toolkit is developed to simplify and speed up the process of developing management packs. It's a combination of guides, recommendations and snippets to make the development easier and let you focus on the design of the management pack instead.
